About Bournemouth Classic Car CLub

Bournemouth classic car club was founded in 1994 by Rudolph Hunt and Jack Callahan. Originally the club was a place for people with classic cars to socialise, but today it has become one of south englands biggest car clubs with a few hundred active members and thousands of atendees at our annual events
